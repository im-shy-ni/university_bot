About Us

Our institution has a longstanding tradition of academic excellence and innovation. For over five decades, we have been a beacon of knowledge, nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders. Our diverse academic programs, modern infrastructure, and experienced faculty ensure that students receive a holistic education that prepares them for both professional and personal success.

We are dedicated to fostering an environment where learning is interactive, research-driven, and globally competitive. Our institution is not just a place of education; it is a community where ideas are nurtured, talents are developed, and lifelong relationships are formed.

Vision

To emerge as a globally recognized hub of higher education, empowering students with knowledge, skills, and values to make a positive impact on society and the world. We strive to be at the forefront of educational advancements, equipping students with the tools to meet the challenges of a rapidly evolving world.

Mission

  • To offer comprehensive academic programs that cater to the changing demands of various industries.
  • To create a student-centric learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and leadership.
  • To engage in cutting-edge research and foster innovation to address real-world challenges.
  • To instill ethical values, social responsibility, and global citizenship in our students.
  • To provide opportunities for holistic personal and professional development through extracurricular and co-curricular activities.

Core Values

  • Academic Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in education, research, and innovation.
  • Integrity and Ethical Responsibility: Upholding honesty, transparency, and fairness in all academic and administrative activities.
  • Diversity and Inclusion: Creating an open, welcoming environment that celebrates different perspectives, cultures, and backgrounds.
  • Community Engagement: Encouraging students to contribute positively to society through outreach programs and social initiatives.
  • Sustainability and Innovation: Promoting eco-friendly practices and fostering a spirit of continuous improvement in technology and education.

Admission

We are excited to welcome students from all backgrounds who are eager to pursue their academic and professional dreams. Our admissions process is straightforward, and we are here to assist you every step of the way.

How to Apply

To apply, simply follow the steps outlined below and submit your application before the deadline.

  • Fill out the online application form.
  • Submit your academic records and any required documents.
  • Pay the application fee (if applicable).
  • Attend an interview or entrance exam (if required).

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completion of secondary education (12th grade or equivalent) for undergraduate courses.
  • A recognized bachelor's degree for postgraduate programs.
  • Additional requirements may apply for specific programs.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We offer various merit-based scholarships and financial aid options to help make education more affordable for our students. Scholarships are awarded based on academic performance and other achievements.

Courses

Our college offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and competencies needed to excel in their careers.

Undergraduate Courses

  • Bachelor of Vocation (B.Voc):
    • Multimedia and Animation: Focuses on creative design, animation techniques, and multimedia production.
    • Business Process and Data Analytics: Emphasizes business operations and data analysis skills.
  • Bachelor of Physical Education (B.P.Ed): A two-year program designed to prepare students for careers in physical education and sports sciences.

Postgraduate Courses

  • Master of Science (M.Sc): Offered in various specializations, providing advanced knowledge and research opportunities in scientific fields.
  • Master of Arts (M.A): Advanced studies in humanities subjects, fostering critical analysis and research skills.
  • Master of Commerce (M.Com): Focuses on advanced accounting, finance, and business management concepts.
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA): A comprehensive program covering leadership, marketing, finance, entrepreneurship, and strategic management.
  • Master of Social Work (MSW): Prepares students for professional practice in social work, emphasizing social justice and community development.
  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA): Focuses on advanced computer science and application development skills.
  • Master of Education (M.Ed): Designed for educators seeking advanced pedagogical knowledge and research skills.

Postgraduate Diploma Courses

  • PG Diploma in Career Guidance for Executives (Online): An online program aimed at providing career counseling skills for professionals.

Doctoral Programs

  • Master of Philosophy (M.Phil): Research-focused program in various disciplines, fostering advanced scholarly inquiry.
  • Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D): Advanced research program aimed at contributing to academic and professional fields.

For more details, visit the official Bharathiar University website.

Fees Structure

Our goal is to provide an affordable yet high-quality education to all our students. Below is an overview of the fees for the upcoming academic year.

Undergraduate Programs

Annual Tuition Fees: $10,000

Additional Fees: Lab fees, registration, and student services fees amounting to $1,500 annually.

Postgraduate Programs

Annual Tuition Fees: $13,000

Additional Fees: Miscellaneous fees including lab fees and registration totaling $2,000 annually.

Services

Our college is committed to supporting students in every aspect of their academic and personal lives. We provide a wide range of services designed to ensure that all students thrive.

Career Counseling

Our career services team is dedicated to helping students achieve their professional goals. We offer resume workshops, mock interviews, and job placement services to ensure that students are well-prepared to enter the workforce upon graduation.

Mental Health Support

We understand the challenges students face during their academic journey. Our counseling services are available to provide emotional support, help manage stress, and promote mental wellness. We also offer workshops on time management and coping strategies.

Student Clubs and Activities

Our campus is home to a variety of student-led clubs and organizations that promote personal growth, leadership, and community involvement. Whether you're interested in sports, arts, or academic clubs, there's something for everyone!
